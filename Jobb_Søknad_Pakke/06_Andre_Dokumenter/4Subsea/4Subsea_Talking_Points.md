## Key Points to Remember

### Always Emphasize
- Your enthusiasm for the technology and industry
- Your ability to learn quickly and adapt
- Your commitment to spending free time mastering new systems and processes
- Your AI experience and how it applies to marketing
- Your understanding of B2B marketing principles
- Your creative and analytical skills
- Quality control and human oversight of AI-generated content
- Understanding of established brand guidelines
- Technical content creation expertise

### Avoid Mentioning
- Salary expectations (unless specifically asked)
- Negative comments about previous employers
- Overly technical jargon without explanation
- Generic marketing approaches that don't fit B2B

### Body Language & Delivery
- Maintain eye contact and confident posture
- Speak clearly and at a measured pace
- Show enthusiasm through tone and gestures
- Listen actively and ask follow-up questions
- Be authentic and genuine in your responses
