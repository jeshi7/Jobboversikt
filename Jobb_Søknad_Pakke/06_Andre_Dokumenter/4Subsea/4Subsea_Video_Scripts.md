## Production Notes for All Videos

### **Visual Style Guidelines:**
- **Color Scheme:** Use 4Subsea brand colors (#003366 primary blue, #0066CC accent blue)
- **Typography:** Clean, professional fonts (Arial Bold for headlines)
- **Graphics:** Technical but accessible, with clear data visualizations
- **Footage:** High-quality shots of offshore operations, technology interfaces, and professional environments

### **Audio Requirements:**
- **Voiceover:** Professional, clear, and engaging
- **Background Music:** Subtle, professional, not distracting
- **Sound Effects:** Minimal, only where they enhance understanding

### **Technical Specifications:**
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (Full HD) minimum
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 for most content, 1:1 for social media versions
- **Duration:** As specified for each video type
- **Format:** MP4 with H.264 encoding

### **Localization Considerations:**
- **Subtitles:** Available in English, Norwegian, Portuguese, and Polish
- **Voiceover:** Consider regional accents for different markets
- **Content:** Adapt examples and case studies for local relevance

### **Distribution Strategy:**
- **Website:** Embedded on relevant pages
- **Social Media:** Optimized versions for LinkedIn, YouTube, and other platforms
- **Email Marketing:** Included in newsletters and follow-up sequences
- **Events:** Used in presentations and booth displays
- **Sales:** Shared with prospects during the sales process
